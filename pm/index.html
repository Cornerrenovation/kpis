Should NPS Be Asked On Calls?

recommended approach
OptionHow it worksUse for KPI?Recommendation
Ask live on callManager asks a simple 0-10 question and logs the score/reason in Airtable.Good for pilotUse for the first month because it is easy to start and fits existing calls.
Send form after callClient receives a 1-minute form with score, reason, and open concern field.Best long-termUse as the official KPI once the habit is created, because it reduces pressure and bias.
Analyze Gemini notes onlyInfer satisfaction from call notes without asking the client directly.NoUse as context only. It is useful for sentiment, but not a real NPS score.
Best setup: ask gently at the end of the call, then send the same question as a follow-up form. If the form is answered, the form score becomes official; if not, the call score is a temporary pilot value.

Call Script

consistent wording for PM calls
MomentQuestionFields to logFollow-up rule
Post-deliveryBased on the delivery experience so far, how likely are you to recommend Corner from 0 to 10?Score, reason, open concern, next action.If 0-8, create recovery task within 1 business day.
Post-installNow that installation is complete, how likely are you to recommend Corner from 0 to 10?Score, reason, unresolved issues, photoshoot readiness.If 9-10 and no open issues, ask for photoshoot approval.
After reclamation resolutionAfter how we handled this issue, how likely are you to recommend Corner from 0 to 10?Score, issue type, resolution date, recovery note.If under 9, review recovery quality with manager.
